Speechcraft Spotlight

Extending Toastmasters' Impact into the Community

60 Minutes
5 Key Strategies
100% Actionable

About This Leadership Lab

Equip leaders with practical tools to implement effective Speechcraft programs

This session equips leaders with practical tools to implement effective Speechcraft programs that attract new members, generate revenue, and extend the positive impact of Toastmasters into the community.

Objective

Provide District 106 leaders with a clear, actionable guide to launching successful Speechcraft programs that boost membership and enhance the district's public profile.

Lab Outline

Welcome & Introduction (5 minutes)

  • Greetings from Ashok Sunkavalli with a focused prompt: Participants share one word that describes the community impact of Toastmasters.
  • Highlight the PQD’s role in promoting Speechcraft as a key strategy for club and district growth, and for fulfilling our community outreach mission.

The Power of Speechcraft (15 minutes)

  • Explain how Speechcraft programs drive membership growth, provide revenue for clubs, and serve as a powerful tool for community engagement.
  • Share a success story of a District 106 club that grew its membership by running a Speechcraft program (real or fictional).
  • Introduce the “Speechcraft Spotlight” framework: Plan, Promote, Perform—to ensure a successful program.

Interactive Speechcraft Design Lab (25 minutes)

  • Break participants into small groups to design a Speechcraft program for a target audience (e.g., a local business, a high school, a community center), focusing on:
    • Plan: Define the program structure, content, and fees.
    • Promote: Brainstorm marketing strategies (e.g., flyers, social media, community partnerships).
    • Perform: Outline the roles, responsibilities, and logistics for running the program.
  • Groups present their program designs in a “Spotlight Pitch,” with Ashok providing feedback on feasibility and alignment with Toastmasters’ best practices.
  • Share a “Speechcraft Toolkit” with a sample agenda, marketing templates, and a financial planning guide.

Scaling Speechcraft Across District 106 (10 minutes)

  • Discuss how Area and Division Directors can encourage and support clubs in launching Speechcraft programs, creating a district-wide initiative.
  • Provide a takeaway: a “Speechcraft Program Guide” with step-by-step instructions and best practices.

Q&A and Call to Action (5 minutes)

  • Address questions on Speechcraft logistics or challenges.
  • Challenge participants to find one potential Speechcraft audience in their area within 30 days and share their findings with the PQD.

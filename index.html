About Your Facilitator

Learn from an experienced Toastmasters leader dedicated to District 106's success

Ashok Sunkavalli serves as the Program Quality Director for District 106, bringing years of Toastmasters experience and leadership expertise to these labs.

His passion for developing leaders and elevating club quality makes him the perfect guide for your leadership journey.

Through these labs, Ashok aims to empower Area Directors, Division Directors, and Club Officers with practical strategies to drive Distinguished Club Program achievements and propel District 106 toward Distinguished status.

Ashok's Leadership Philosophy

  • Empower through practical, actionable strategies
  • Foster collaboration and shared learning
  • Focus on measurable results and district goals
  • Celebrate successes along the leadership journey

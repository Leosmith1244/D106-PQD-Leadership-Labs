Pathways Power-Up

Igniting Member Success with Dynamic Leadership

60 Minutes
5 Key Strategies
100% Actionable

About This Leadership Lab

Equip yourself with innovative tools to promote Pathways adoption and enhance member progress

This session inspires leaders to champion the Pathways educational program with creative, high-energy strategies to boost member engagement, accelerate educational awards, and drive DCP success.

Objective

Equip District 106 leaders with innovative tools to promote Pathways adoption, enhance member progress, and support the district's goal of achieving Distinguished status.

Lab Outline

A comprehensive 60-minute session packed with actionable strategies

Welcome & Introduction

5 minutes

Greetings from Ashok Sunkavalli with a high-energy icebreaker: Participants share one word describing their vision for Pathways success in District 106.

Highlight the Program Quality Director's role in elevating club and district success through Pathways.

Why Pathways Matters

15 minutes

Showcase how Pathways drives member growth and club success, using District 106 examples.

Share an inspiring story of a District 106 member who transformed through Pathways.

Introduce the "Pathways Power-Up" framework: Engage, Simplify, Celebrate.

Interactive Workshop

25 minutes

Break into small groups to brainstorm creative ways to "power up" Pathways in their clubs.

Groups present ideas in a high-energy "pitch-off" with feedback from Ashok.

Live demonstration of "Pathways Hacks" to simplify leadership tasks.

Action Plan & Impact

10 minutes

Discuss how Area and Division Directors can promote strategies across District 106.

Provide a "Pathways Power-Up Playbook" with templates and tracking tools.

Highlight how efforts align with elevating District 106 to Distinguished status.

Q&A and Call to Action

5 minutes

Open the floor for questions on Pathways challenges or implementation.

Challenge participants to implement one "Power-Up" strategy within 30 days.
